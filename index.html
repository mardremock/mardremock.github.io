The American Crow

American crows are part of the genus Corvus.

Here are some cool things that crows can do:

  • They can recognize individual human faces.
  • They understand the physics of water displacement.
  • They drop nuts onto cars' windshields, and when the crosswalk sign is on and no cars are coming, they fly down to pick them up.
  • They're songbirds, even though they eat other songbirds.
